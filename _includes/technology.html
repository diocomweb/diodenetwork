Technology

/01

Permissionless Infrastructure

Anyone can bring a Diode Node online and contribute to the future of Internet security. Every activated country, region, city, and facility provides local end-to-end encrypted communications for everyone in region.

/02

Commodity Bandwidth Pricing

You can use the Diode Network for the cost of the bandwidth your application / use-case consumes. Many nodes will accept bandwidth at a margin to the commodity cost of bandwidth - which is from $0.02 - $0.10/GB.

/03

Enterprise Deployments

Enterprise Relay Nodes can be deployed that are dedicated to your application. Enterprise nodes enjoy the security of the full network while allowing only authorized traffic. Contact Diode for more details.

/04

Best Regional Availability

The Diode Network is available in hundreds of locations thanks to its decentralized and mathematically secured infrastructure. Diode Network users have global access in every region the network operates.

/05

No Supplier Risk

Group and identity relationships are vulnerable in most Zero Trust networks. This introduces supplier risk that cannot be ignored. Diode delivers better security than self-custody infrastructure, with all of the convenience of a SaaS network.

/05

Self Custody Identities

Diode clients use Ethereum public addresses as their identity and routing address. These are self-custody credentials that enable a full life cycle of identity management and enterprise integrations.

/06

Mathematical Security

Where other networks have cyber, physical, and insider risks, the Diode Network’s infrastructure is mathematically secure via smart contracts and the Blockquick™ family of reputation algorithms.

/07

Regulatory Compliance

Leave your existing assets in place, and extend with Zero Trust connectivity. The Diode Network's end-to-end encryption can be integrated into most regulatory regimes - including HIPPA, SOC 2, ISO 27001, and others.

/08

SaaS Applications

Leverage the permissionles nature of the Diode Network security by building and deploying SaaS apps. You pay only for the bandwidth your app consumes - no Diode account or billing relationship is required!

/09

Blockchain Name System

Diode’s Blockchain Name System (BNS) allows you to assign a friendly name to any client, identity, or domain. It provides connectivity, routing, and communication capabilities for application usernames.
