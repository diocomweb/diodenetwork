Technology

/01

Permissionless Infrastructure

Anyone can bring a Diode Node online and contribute to the future of Internet security. Every activated country, region, city, and facility provides local end-to-end encrypted communications for everyone in region.

/02

Commodity Bandwidth Pricing

You can use the Diode Network for the cost of the bandwidth you use.Many nodes will accept bandwidth that provides a margin to the commodity cost - which is from $0.02 - $0.10/GB depending on the region.

/03

Self Custody Identities

Diode clients use Ethereum public addresses as their identity and routing address. These are self-custody credentials that enable a full life cycle of identity management and enterprise integrations.

/03

Best Regional Availability

The Diode Network is available in over 120 locations thanks to its decentralized and mathematically secured infrastructure. Diode Network users have global access in every region the network operates.

/03

Blockchain Name System

Diode’s Blockchain Name System (BNS) allows you to assign a friendly name to any client, identity, or domain. It provides connectivity, routing, and communication capabilities for application usernames.

/03

Mathematical Security

Where other networks are vulnerable, the Diode Network’s infrastructure is mathematically secure - it eliminates physical, cyber, and insider threats from the equation.

/03

Regulatory Compliance

The Diode Network is a an end-to-end encryption communication network that can be integrated into many regulatory regimes - including HIPPA, SOC 2, ISO 27001, and others.

/03

SaaS Applications

Thanks to the permissionles nature of the network, no Diode account or billing relationship is required to build or deploy SaaS apps.

/03

Enterprise Deployments

Contact us to deploy an Enterprise node that is dedicated to your application. Enterprise nodes enjoy the security of the full network while actively filtering authorized traffic.
